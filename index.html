About Us

We are dedicated to improving the environment by planting trees in Miami. Our mission is to create a greener, healthier city for all residents and visitors. We believe in the power of community and the importance of engaging with local residents to make a lasting impact. By working together, we can transform urban spaces into lush, green areas that provide numerous benefits for both people and wildlife.

Our organization was founded by a group of passionate environmentalists who saw the need for more green spaces in Miami. Over the years, we have grown into a dedicated team of volunteers and supporters who share our vision of a greener city. We have partnered with local schools, businesses, and government agencies to plant thousands of trees across Miami, creating beautiful, sustainable environments for future generations to enjoy.

Our tree planting initiatives focus on areas that are most in need of green spaces, such as underserved neighborhoods and urban heat islands. By strategically planting trees in these areas, we can help reduce air pollution, lower temperatures, and improve overall quality of life for residents. In addition to tree planting, we also provide education and resources to help the community understand the importance of trees and how to care for them.

Join us in our mission to make Miami a greener, healthier place to live. Whether you want to volunteer your time, donate to support our efforts, or simply learn more about the benefits of trees, we welcome you to be a part of our growing community.

Get Involved

Volunteer

Volunteer at a tree planting event and help us make Miami greener. Your time and effort can make a significant impact on our community and the environment. Whether you are an individual, a group, or a corporate team, we welcome everyone to join us in our mission. Our volunteer events are fun, educational, and a great way to meet like-minded people who care about the environment.

Donate

Donate to support our initiatives and help us plant more trees. Your financial contributions go directly toward purchasing trees, soil amendments, and necessary tools. Additionally, your support helps fund our educational programs and community outreach efforts. Donations of any size are greatly appreciated and make a tangible difference in our ability to create a greener Miami.

Spread the Word

Share our mission on social media and educate others about the importance of trees. Help us reach a wider audience and inspire more people to join our cause. By spreading the word, you can amplify our impact and encourage others to get involved in tree planting and environmental stewardship. Follow us on social media, share our posts, and invite your friends and family to join our community.

Educate

Educate others about the importance of trees and how to care for them. Join our educational programs and community outreach efforts to promote environmental stewardship. We offer workshops, seminars, and school programs that teach people of all ages about the benefits of trees and how to care for them. By raising awareness and providing knowledge, we can empower more people to take action and make a positive impact on the environment.
