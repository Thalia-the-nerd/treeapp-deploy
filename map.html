Tree Planting Locations

Amelia Earhart Park

Amelia Earhart Park is a 515-acre urban park in Miami-Dade County, Florida. It features a variety of recreational amenities including bike trails, a petting zoo, and a lake for fishing and paddle boating.

West Little River Park

West Little River Park is a community park located in Miami-Dade County, Florida. It offers a variety of amenities including playgrounds, sports fields, and picnic areas.

Northwest 17th Avenue Park

Northwest 17th Avenue Park is a small community park located in Miami-Dade County, Florida. It offers a playground and open green spaces for recreational activities.
