Overtown Community Garden

950 NW 3rd Ave, Miami, FL 33136

About This Location

Overtown Community Garden is a community-driven garden in the heart of Overtown. This location is perfect for fruit trees and provides an opportunity to engage with local residents. The garden was established in 2018 as part of an initiative to bring fresh produce and green spaces to the Overtown neighborhood.

Why This Location?

This location was selected for tree planting because:

  • The soil has been prepared and enriched for growing plants
  • The garden is maintained by local volunteers, ensuring trees will be cared for
  • Fruit trees will provide both shade and food for the community
  • The location is central to the Overtown neighborhood, making it accessible to residents
  • Adding trees will enhance the garden's beauty and educational value

Recommended Tree Species

Based on the soil conditions and climate, we recommend planting the following tree species at this location:

  • Mango (Mangifera indica) - Provides delicious fruit and shade
  • Avocado (Persea americana) - Popular fruit tree with dense canopy
  • Loquat (Eriobotrya japonica) - Small tree with edible fruit
  • Guava (Psidium guajava) - Hardy tree with fragrant fruit
  • Mulberry (Morus spp.) - Fast-growing with sweet berries
