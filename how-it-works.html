How It Works

Explore Locations

Discover various locations in Miami where you can plant a tree. We have identified key areas that would benefit from increased green spaces, including urban neighborhoods, parks, and school grounds. By planting trees in these locations, we can help create a more balanced and sustainable urban environment. Each location is carefully selected based on its potential to provide environmental, social, and economic benefits to the community.

Choose a Tree

Select the type of tree that best suits the location and your preferences. We offer a variety of native and climate-appropriate tree species to ensure successful growth and long-term sustainability. Our team can provide guidance on the best tree species for your chosen location based on soil type, sunlight, and water availability. By choosing the right tree, you can help create a thriving ecosystem that supports local wildlife and enhances the beauty of the area.

Make a Donation

Your contribution helps cover the costs of planting, maintenance, and monitoring. Donations go directly towards purchasing trees, soil amendments, and necessary tools. Additionally, your support helps fund educational programs and community outreach efforts to promote environmental stewardship. By donating, you are making a tangible difference in our ability to create a greener, healthier Miami for current and future generations.

We Plant

Our dedicated team will plant the tree and take care of its initial growth needs. We ensure that each tree is properly planted and receives the necessary care during its critical establishment period. This includes regular watering, mulching, and protection from pests and diseases. By providing the right care, we can help each tree thrive and reach its full potential, contributing to a healthier and more sustainable environment.

Enjoy the Impact

Visit your tree and enjoy the positive impact you've made on Miami's environment! Our team will keep you updated on the progress of your tree, and you are welcome to visit and see the difference you have made. Trees provide numerous benefits, including improved air quality, reduced urban heat, and enhanced beauty and biodiversity. By planting a tree, you are leaving a lasting legacy that will benefit the community for years to come.
