Tree Care Guide

Watering

New trees need regular watering. We'll provide a customized watering schedule based on your tree species and local conditions.

  • First year: Water 2-3 times per week
  • Second year: Water 1-2 times per week
  • After establishment: Water during dry spells

Pruning

Regular pruning helps maintain tree health and structure. Our experts will guide you through proper pruning techniques.

  • Remove dead or damaged branches
  • Maintain proper spacing between branches
  • Follow species-specific pruning guidelines

Fertilization

Proper nutrition ensures healthy growth. We'll help you choose the right fertilizer and application schedule.

  • Use slow-release fertilizers
  • Apply in early spring or fall
  • Follow package instructions carefully

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of trees do you plant?

We focus on native and climate-appropriate trees that are well-suited to Miami's environment. Each tree is carefully selected based on local conditions and community needs.

How do I choose the right location?

Our interactive map helps you identify suitable locations based on soil type, sunlight, and space availability. We also consider underground utilities and future growth space.

What happens after I plant a tree?

You'll receive regular care reminders and can track your tree's growth through our app. Our team provides ongoing support and maintenance guidance.

Can I plant a tree in memory of someone?

Yes! We offer a special memorial tree program where you can dedicate a tree to a loved one. You can add photos, stories, and memories to your tree's digital profile.

What You Need to Know

Time Commitment

Planting typically takes 1-2 hours, including orientation and cleanup. Follow-up care is minimal but important for tree survival.

Equipment

We provide all necessary tools and materials. Just bring comfortable clothes, closed-toe shoes, and your enthusiasm!

Group Size

Planting events can accommodate individuals or groups. Family and friends are welcome to join!

Certification

No prior experience needed. Our experts will train you in proper tree planting techniques.
