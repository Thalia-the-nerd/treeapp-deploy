How TreePlace.App Works

Our simple process makes it easy to plant trees and make Miami greener.

How TreePlace.App Works

1

Choose a Location

Browse our interactive map to find the perfect spot for your tree. We'll help you identify suitable locations based on soil type, sunlight, and space availability.

2

Select Your Tree

Choose from our curated selection of native and climate-appropriate trees. Each tree comes with detailed information about its growth requirements and benefits.

3

Schedule Planting

Pick a date that works for you and join our community planting event. Our experts will guide you through the entire process.

4

Plant Together

Join our community of volunteers and plant your tree. We'll provide all the necessary tools and guidance to ensure successful planting.

5

Track Growth

Monitor your tree's growth through our app. Receive care reminders and connect with other tree planters in your area.

What You Need to Know

Time Commitment

Planting typically takes 1-2 hours, including orientation and cleanup. Follow-up care is minimal but important for tree survival.

Equipment

We provide all necessary tools and materials. Just bring comfortable clothes, closed-toe shoes, and your enthusiasm!

Group Size

Planting events can accommodate individuals or groups. Family and friends are welcome to join!

Certification

No prior experience needed. Our experts will train you in proper tree planting techniques.
